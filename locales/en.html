Welcome to Parentis‑en‑Born

Your stay between lake and ocean in the Landes

Discover the Landes

Parentis‑en‑Born offers an exceptional setting in the heart of the Landes pine forest, between the large lake and the Atlantic Ocean.

🌊 The Atlantic Ocean

Only 20 km away — enjoy the beaches of Biscarrosse and Mimizan. Surfing, swimming and long sandy walks await.

🏞️ Lake Parentis

One of the largest lakes in the Landes (3600 ha), warm shallow waters ideal for swimming and water sports.

🌲 The Landes Forest

Explore the majestic pine forest, the Landes de Gascogne Regional Park, and cycle the Vélodyssée on safe paths.
